About Us

Built on Conviction,
Powered by Connection

In 2018, Leo and Anna left their roles as senior partners at Fortune 500 firms to found UIChain. They understood how the world's largest companies operate — and saw firsthand the resource gap that left small and mid-sized businesses behind in brand expansion, KOL partnerships, and global sourcing.

UIChain's mission has never changed: to give every SME access to world-class resources, tools, and expertise — on equal footing.

Founded — Leo and Anna co-founded UIChain, focused on driving growth for global SMEs through brand strategy and global connections
